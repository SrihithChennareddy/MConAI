Explanation:

   - AD → AD: 15

2. Off-diagonal cells (misclassifications):
   - AD → MCI: 3, AD → HC: 2
   - MCI → AD: 1, MCI → HC: 3

   - AD: 15 + 3 + 2 = 20 ✅

   Accuracy (overall):
   (15 + 13 + 33) / (20 + 17 + 39) = 61 / 76 ≈ 0.803

   Recall per class:
   - AD recall = 15 / 20 = 0.75
   - MCI recall = 13 / 17 ≈ 0.765
   - HC recall = 33 / 39 ≈ 0.846

   Precision per class:
   - AD precision = 15 / (15 + 1 + 1) = 15 / 17 ≈ 0.882
   - MCI precision = 13 / (3 + 13 + 5) = 13 / 21 ≈ 0.619
   - HC precision = 33 / (2 + 3 + 33) = 33 / 38 ≈ 0.868

5. Key insights:
   - HC is classified well (high recall & precision)
   - AD mostly accurate; small confusion with MCI and HC
